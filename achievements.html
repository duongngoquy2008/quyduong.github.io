Academic awards and achievements

Ever since I was young, I have developed a great passion for math ever since I was in primary school. I have participated in several math competitions and gained some prizes and medal, throughout secondary and high school.

Third prize in Nation Math Olympiad 2025

In my high school education, I chose to participate in math competitions to keep pursuing my passion for studying math. The third prize achieved in the Olympiad is one of my biggest achievements during high school.

Twice recognized as an Outstanding Student of the Capital (2024 & 2025)

First Prize in Hanoi National University High School Olympiad

The competition occurred in October 2024. I got the First Prize with a Perfect Score of 20/20.

Gold Medal in the 18th Hung Vuong Camp in 2024

The competition is held every year, aiming to create a playground for students passionate about Mathematics. Most students participating in the camp were from specialized high schools in Northern Midland Vietnam and mountainous areas.

Gold Medal (Math) and Bronze Medal (Science) in International Stem Olympiad 2024 held in The Hague, The Netherlands

Third Prize in National Olympiad Mathematics for Students and Pupils in 2024

This competition is held by Vietnam Mathematical Society, one of the biggest organizations working in Mathematics in Vietnam.

First Prize in City-level Mathematics Competition in 2023

This competition occurred in September 2023, only 2 months after I got into high school. Getting first prize after only 2 months of learning made this one of my proudest achievements.

Valedictorian of HUS High School for Gifted Students Entrance Exam in 2023

My initial specialized subjects were Physics, in grade 6, 7, and Chemistry, in grade 8. However, by the end of grade 8, I realized that Math is my real passion and decided to choose Math as my chosen specialized subject. For me, it was one of the turning points in my academic journey. After one year of learning and practicing, I became the Valedictorian in the entrance math exam of HUS HSGS, which I believe is one of my proudest achievements.
